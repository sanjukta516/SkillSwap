How SkillSwap Works

  1. Add Your Skills: Go to Add Skill and fill in your name, email, year/department, the skill you can teach, and the skill you want to learn.
  2. Browse Skills: Use the Browse page to see what others can teach and what they want to learn. Use the search bar to filter results.
  3. Find Matches: On the Matches page, SkillSwap automatically finds people who can teach you what you want to learn, and who want to learn what you can teach.
  4. Reach Out: Each match shows the names, skills, and emails of users. Use the email shown to directly contact them and start learning/teaching together.
